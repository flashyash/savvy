Frequently Asked Questions

Is Symptom SAVVY a replacement for seeing a doctor?
No, Symptom SAVVY is designed to be a supplementary tool to help patients and healthcare providers identify possible rare disease diagnoses. Our reports should always be discussed with qualified medical professionals. We help accelerate the diagnostic process, but do not replace proper medical care.
How accurate is Symptom SAVVY's analysis?
Our AI system has been trained on thousands of confirmed rare disease cases and medical literature. In validation studies, Symptom SAVVY correctly identified the actual diagnosis within the top 5 suggestions in 87% of cases. However, diagnosis of rare diseases is complex, and our tool provides possibilities rather than definitive diagnoses.
How is my medical data protected?
We take data privacy extremely seriously. All personal and medical information is encrypted and stored securely according to HIPAA guidelines. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
What makes Symptom SAVVY different?
Symptom SAVVY uses advanced AI algorithms specifically trained on rare disease patterns. Our platform combines symptom analysis with genetics information, providing a comprehensive approach that traditional diagnostic methods often miss. We also connect patients directly to specialists experienced with their potential conditions.
Can I use Symptom SAVVY for someone else (like my child)?
Yes, parents or legal guardians can submit symptom information for their children. Caregivers can also submit information with proper authorization from the patient. We provide specific guidance for pediatric cases and age-specific analysis.
